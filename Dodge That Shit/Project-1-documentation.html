There are only 5 buttons involved in our application. Movement keys (w,s,a,d) and space.

Through our simple controls and onboarding the game can be easily picked up.

Yes.

14 minutes of unique background music. Sound effects for multiplier pickups, multiplier removal and end of game.

All of the controls are clearly displayed on the screen at the beginning of each game. The game will not begin until the player presses each of them. Multipliers are also displayed before the player encounters obstacles and make a rewarding noise when aquired.

We don't punish players. The game slowly gets harder, through increasing speed of obstacles and spawn times. There are also no game breaking bugs.

The high score is displayed at the end of game and stored via local storage.

At first the game is just about surviving. Eventually you begin to learn strategies such as picking up a multiplier just before any other powerups to maximize the powerup. You learn how to effectively use the space bar in order to earn maxiumum points.
